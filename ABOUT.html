About Our Brand

Born from restless nights and endless dreams, Insomnia is more than a clothing brand — it’s a lifestyle. Created by one mind that refuses to sleep, Insomnia stands for those who live when the world goes quiet. The night is where ideas spark, music hits deeper, and style speaks louder. Every piece is made to capture that energy — raw, real, and unapologetic. We don’t chase trends; we live moments. Insomnia is for the ones who never clock out, who move through the darkness chasing light and living life to the fullest. Stay awake. Stay alive. Live Insomnia.
